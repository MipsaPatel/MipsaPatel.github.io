About Me

I am currently working as a Software Engineer at LinkedIn, Bangalore. As part of the Artificial Intelligence team, I work on improving the quality of job recommendations for low and medium skilled members. I have also worked on internal tools and pipelines to automate the process of collecting labelled data through crowdsourcing.

I received my Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) degree from the Computer Science and Engineering department of Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore (2014 - 2018).
